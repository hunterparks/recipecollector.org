Buffalo Chicken Dip 🐔

A creamy, spicy dip that is suitable for any party!

Prep: 5 mins | Total: 80 mins | Yield: 12 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pound - Shredded Chicken, with salt, pepper, and garlic
  • ¼ cup - Ranch Dressing
  • ¼ cup - Buffalo Sauce
  • 1 - Cream Cheese, 8 ounce block
  • 1 cup - White Cheddar, shredded
  • 1 cup - Gouda, shredded

Directions

  1. Mix ingredients into alumnium pan. Put into 250* smoker for one hour.
  2. Stir, and add a little bit more buffalo sauce.
  3. Return to smoker and cook for another 15 minutes.
  4. Enjoy!
