Italian Penicilin

Prep: 0 mins | Total: 0 mins | Yield: X servings

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 6 cups water
  • 3 celery stalks chopped up
  • 3 carrots peeled and cut
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion

Directions

  1. mix ingredients in pot
  2. once boiling, set to medium heat and simmer for 45 minutes
  3. scoop out the stuff and put into blender, add boullion cube, and a scoop of the broth - blend until smooth
  4. slowly pour mixture back into broth
  5. spoon broth mixture onto orzo, top with parmesan cheese
