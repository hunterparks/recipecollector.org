yield: "4 servings"
ingredients:
    - "2 teaspoons - Sesame oil"
    - "800 grams - Chicken thigh fillets, halved"
    - "½ cup (160 grams) - Peach & mango jam"
    - "⅓ cup (80 milliliters) - Barbecue sauce"
    - "⅓ cup (80 milliliters) - Pineapple juice, sweetened"
    - "2 cloves - Garlic, finely chopped"
    - "1 - Brown onion, small, halved, thinly sliced"
    - "2 tablespoons - Soy sauce"
    - "2 - Green shallots, sliced diagonally"
    - "½ teaspoon - Sesame seeds"
    - "White rice, steamed, to serve"
directions:
    - "Heat the oil in a large deep frying pan over medium-high
      heat. Cook the chicken for 4-5 minutes each side or until
      browned."
    - "Meanwhile, combine the jam, barbecue sauce, pineapple
      juice, garlic, onion, soy and 1 tsp freshly cracked black
      pepper in a bowl."
    - "Pour jam mixture over chicken and bring to a simmer.
      Reduce heat to medium-low. Cook, covered, turning
      chicken halfway, for 15 minutes. Uncover and cook, basting
      chicken with jam mixture, for a further 5 minutes or until
      chicken is cooked through and sauce has thickened."
    - "Sprinkle chicken with shallot and sesame seeds. Serve
      with rice. Watch our step-by-step video below to see how
      to make this Sticky Honolulu chicken recipe."
