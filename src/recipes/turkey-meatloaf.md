prepTime: 10
totalTime: 55
yield: "4 servings"
ingredients:
  - "Meatloaf:"
  - "1 lb - 90% lean ground turkey, or 1 lb ground beef and 1 lb ground turkey"
  - "¼ cup - Panko breadcrumbs"
  - "⅛ cup - Whole milk, or beef broth, plus more as needed"
  - "1 large - Egg, whisked"
  - "1-1½ tbsp - Fresh parsley, or 2 tsp dried parsley"
  - "1 tbsp - Worcestershire sauce"
  - "1 tbsp - Ketchup"
  - "⅝ tsp - Kosher salt"
  - "⅛ tsp - Ground black pepper"
  - "½ tsp - Garlic powder"
  - "½ tsp - Onion powder"
  - "Sauce:"
  - "¼ cup - Ketchup or BBQ sauce (or half and half)"
  - "1 tbsp - Light brown sugar"
directions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 375°F. Line a baking sheet or 9x5-inch loaf pan with foil. Lightly spray with nonstick spray and set aside."
  - "In a large bowl, mix the turkey, breadcrumbs, milk, egg, parsley, Worcestershire sauce, ketchup, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder until combined. Don't overmix or the meat won't be as tender."
  - "Form the mixture into a loaf shape on the baking sheet. In a small bowl, mix together the ketchup and/or BBQ sauce and brown sugar. Pour over the top of the meatloaf. Add pepper on top, if desired."
  - "Bake 45 to 55 minutes, or until the center reaches 160°F. Allow to rest 5 to 10 minutes before slicing into 8 to 10 slices."
notes:
  - "You may substitute fresh onion and garlic (½ cup onion, 4 garlic cloves minced), but sauté them with a little olive oil before adding to the meat."
  - "For a little heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the meat or a few dashes of chili sauce or sriracha to the sauce."
  - "To make gluten free, use gluten free breadcrumbs or old fashioned oats."
  - "For extra saucy meatloaf, mix up another batch of sauce and brush it on during the last 10 minutes of cooking, or use it for dipping."
  - "Tip: Use good quality ground turkey for optimal flavor."
